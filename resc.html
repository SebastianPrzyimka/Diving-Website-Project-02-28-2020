Rescue Diver

Why is it worth it? The PADI RESCUE DIVER diving course will deepen your knowledge of diving safety and diving rescue, Teach you how to plan a safe dive for you and your partner, They will familiarize you with diving rescue equipment and learn how to react properly during a diving accident.

What do you need to try? Minimum age: 15 years old, Diving equipment, which you will receive as part of the price of the course at the Diving Center: diving cylinder with a single or double valve with a capacity of 10l. , or 12l, or 15l. and a weight belt with a ballast suitable for a diving suit. Diving equipment own or rented: mask, snorkel, strip fins, 5 mm diving boots, 5 mm diving gloves, 5 mm or 7 mm diving suit + 5 mm or 7 mm warmer with hood, diving jacket, breathing machine in REC configuration. a weight belt that is included in the price of the PADI Rescue Diver course PADI Advanced Open Water Diver Certificate (PADI AOWD) or another organization's certificate - [see list], Document certifying the completion of the EDF PADI first aid course in the last 24 months.

What will you do? The PADI RESCUE DIVER diving course will prepare you to anticipate potential problems that may occur during the dive and to solve them. During this course you will learn: Rescue Diver course how to control stress under water and how to help a diver who is under stress, first aid during the dive, about first aid equipment, technique of pulling an injured diver out of the water, how to help a panicked diver, identification of problems during the dive, procedures for finding a lost diver underwater, providing first aid when towing an unconscious diver on the surface, artificial respiration while towing on the surface, evacuation of an injured diver from water, providing first aid to injured divers during the dive, conduct during simulated diving accident scenarios.

How long does this course take? Recommended number of hours: 25h, minimum two days in open waters.

What do you need? Training materials - you will receive in the Diving Center. Diving equipment. Logbook (dive book / dive record). Doctor's statement that there are no contraindications for diving.

What will you get after this course? Ability to deal with a diving accident or emergency situation when diving in open waters. If you have completed the PADI RESCUE DIVER diving course, you can join the first professional PADI Divemaster diving course or apply for the prestigious PADI Master Scuba Diver (PADI MSD).
